See this fun picture of a human next to these words? Well, that’d be me, Jennifer Holcombe. Though I may look like a general badass who takes no sass, inside I’m warm and sweet like an apple pie. It probably stems from my Southern upbringing... Georgia peach, born and raised. Go Braves! But don’t let my warmth fool you, I’m also one who takes pride in hard work and LIVES for being busy. My MFA in Acting from UC Irvine and BFA in Musical Theater will prove that to you. But don’t trust my degrees alone, feel free to ask any one of the amazing people I’ve had the pleasure to work with. That’s the reason I do this work... to collaborate with talented, passionate artists who care about changing the world through creative endeavors. So let’s make some art!

Or, if you’re sick of making art, we can also indulge in any of my other favorite things... let’s list them Julie Andrews’ style, shall we?
